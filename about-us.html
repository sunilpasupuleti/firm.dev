About us

What are we ?


Our Story

Firm.dev, an abbreviation for firmware developers, started in 2020 amidst pandemic, when our beginning steps were to use technology in solving pandemic induced challenges.
From the humble beginning then, our hand-picked team of engineers, designers and developers today provide services ranging from IoT (Internet of Things), embedded system, firmware development, electronic design, printed circuit board (PCB) designing and manufacturing.


Our Services

Our platform caters for all our customers requirements. From product ideation to proof of concept, prototype designing to the end product, we’re here to handle it all.
Helping people materialise their ideas into the products through analysis, deliberation, experimentation, designing and manufacturing. Further, testing the ideation and checking if it actually works and assisting in better design changes. Working product, electronics design, PCB manufacturing, mechanical CAD and finished product capable of public use and client are all dealt with here. Our aim for excelling in our services is to provide hassle-free journey for our customers with smooth conversations free from any glitches.

Future Scope

We aspire to continue to use our team’s hand-on experience in building firmware, PCBs, embedded systems and IoT related products from scratch. Our manufacturing services should cater the needs of the clients world-over and the synergy of our teams should continue delivering world class products.
Apart from being firmware developers, we have been closely working for our budding technology enthusiasts who are curious to know it all, experiment it all and try it all. Understanding their needs, we branched a dedicated platform PCB Cupid for these geeks. We strive to make this platform a global PCB one-in-all channel to be used by experts, students, engineers, enthusiasts, manufacturers and others to engage and share their knowledge.

Ethics

At firm.dev, with clarity of our goals and vision, we adhere to professionalism, respect for the work and fellow teammates, integrity, discipline and dedication to our client’s requirements.
The satisfaction of our clients is all that matters the most to each one of our teammates.
Further we encourage feedback that ensures a two way communication between us and you, thus fueling our spirits to strive for excellence in our services.
